PIG DICE

Instructions

Welcome to the game of pig dice

1. The aim of the game is to find the first player that accumulates a score of 100.

2. Player One rolls the dice for as many times as they want, accumulating a the score in the process, with the catch being rolling a one and losing their turn.

3. When the player is satisfied with their score, they choose Hold and their current score is saved. The turn is passed to the next player.

4. The turn is passed until a player gets to 100 and wins.
